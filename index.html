About us

We are going through difficult times due to the pandemic and it was with this in mind that this token was created in order to raise a certain % in money to help people in need, such as homeless people and people with disabilities. Something essential for people, for example: a bread roll, a plate of food, warm clothes, blankets and even hygiene products.

For people with some type of disability, for example: a crutch, a walker, a mattress or pillow, a simple wheelchair or even a motorized wheelchair.

All of this ends up becoming something very difficult to acquire due to its values ​​being very high and most of the times it ends up being an almost impossible mission!
