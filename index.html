About us

We are going through difficult times due to the pandemic, it was with this in mind that this project was created in order to raise a certain % in money to help people in need, such as homeless people and people with disabilities. This Token will distribute in each transaction, 5% for marketing, 6% rewards in Ethereum for holders and 9% to help homeless people and people with any kind of disability.

This project will benefit people in need with something so simple and essential, but at the same time so difficult for them.

For example: Helping homeless people to buy a bread roll, a plate of food, warm clothes, blankets and even products for their personal hygiene.

For people with a disability It helps to buy a crutch, a walker, a mattress, a bed sores, a simple wheelchair or even a motorized wheelchair.

All of this ends up becoming something very difficult to acquire due to its values ​​being very high and most of the times it ends up being an almost impossible mission!
